Uttarakhand's Local Cuisine

Uttarakhand's cuisine is simple, nutritious, and perfectly suited to the mountainous terrain. The food is primarily prepared with locally grown ingredients and is known for its unique flavors and health benefits.

Traditional dishes are often cooked over wood fires, enhancing their natural flavors. From the protein-rich dishes of the Garhwal region to the sweet delicacies of Kumaon, Uttarakhand's food culture is as diverse as its landscape.
